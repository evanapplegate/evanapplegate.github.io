Class 1 violations: imminent danger to clients or a substantial probability that death or serious physical or emotional harm would result. These violations must be corrected within 24 hours. Carries a mandatory $5-10k fine.
Class 2 violations: directly threaten the physical or emotional health, safety or security of clients (other than Class I). Carries a mandatory $1k-$5k fine.
