Assisted living data: Florida Agency for Health Care Administration – Assisted Living Facilities Summary. Data period: June 16, 2018 to June 15, 2023
Class I violations: imminent danger to clients or a substantial probability that death or serious physical or emotional harm would result. These violations must be corrected within 24 hours. Carries a mandatory $5-10k fine.
Class II violations: directly threaten the physical or emotional health, safety or security of clients (other than Class I). Carries a mandatory $1k-$5k fine.
